About me

Hi! My name is Kirsten, you can call me Kira. As you can probably guess by now, I am a web developer. I've always loved playing around with code - I even created Myspace layouts and 'Glittermojis' at 12 years old. Of course, that's irrelevant - I will give a brief overview of my serious coding journey to this day.
I began studying some personal hobbies more intensively, but unfortunately, most of the study resource sites I'd found looked as if they hadn't been touched since the 90's web days. I can't explain exactly what came over me, but I decided to try my hand at making my own personal study site which would appeal to me and also help me to learn as I created it. Soon after, I began realizing how much fun it is to 'make things.' On my own, I progressively began incorporating coding into my daily life.
I started learning different languages and libraries / frameworks to compliment my personal projects. Naturally, coding began taking priority over my personal hobbies as I fell in love with it more and more.

To this moment, I have now compiled a large set of personal projects to build upon and showcase my knowledge in the field. My technical knowledge is as so: Front end language / framework skills: Javascript, HTML & CSS, React.js, jQuery, and Bootstrap.
Back end language / framework skills: Java, Node.js, Express, Django, and Rails. Also experienced with Git. I am seeking to build upon my current experience and learn more everyday with a company that will allow me to work with familiar and new challenges, and gain more skills.
Both my work life and mental state are 100% ready to take the plunge into the technical work field using my hard-earned web development skills.
I hope to prove myself and my work skills.
